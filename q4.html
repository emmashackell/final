Comparison of PNG vs JPG

Pros of PNG Compared to JPG

  • Supports lossless compression, preserving image quality
  • Supports transparent backgrounds (alpha channel)
  • Better for images with sharp edges or text
  • Widely supported across web platforms and applications
  • Suitable for images that require high quality, such as logos

Cons of PNG Compared to JPG

  • File sizes are generally larger than JPGs, making them slower to load
  • Not ideal for complex images like photographs due to large file size
  • Does not support as many color depths as JPG (limited to 24-bit)
  • Not as efficient for web use in terms of storage and bandwidth
  • May cause longer load times for web pages with many high-quality images
